About

If you have ever had to figure out who the carrier of a phone number is, you know how stressful it might be.
Many people cram the prefixes of a certain network to know which is which but how many things person wan put for head? Especially if you have a 512kb RAM brain like me.

This site works on that same principle by checking the prefix.
However, since the implementation of mobile number portability in Nigeria in 2013, the number prefix can no longer be used to reliably determine the operator.[1]
Hence, it is very likely that the results generated might be inaccurate.
Nevertheless, since only a little percentage of the population ports from a provider to the other, there remains considerable confidence in the accuracy of results.
